Medical Services

Our Services

Explore the various Korean medicine services offered at Bit Korean Medicine Clinic.
From pain treatment to beauty care, we provide professional service.

🪡

Acupuncture

Fine needles placed at acupoints regulate the flow of Qi and blood to relieve pain. Effective for musculoskeletal conditions, headaches, digestive disorders, insomnia, and fatigue.

Learn More →
💉

Pharmacopuncture

Refined herbal medicine extracts injected into acupoints, combining acupuncture's stimulation with the pharmacological effects of herbal medicine for enhanced results.

Learn More →
🤲

Chuna Therapy

Korean manual therapy that corrects the spine and joints by hand. Effective for herniated discs, scoliosis, pelvic imbalance, and postural corrections.

Learn More →
🔥

Cupping Therapy

Suction applied to the skin promotes blood circulation and helps remove metabolic waste. Excellent for muscle fatigue and shoulder/back tension.

Learn More →
⚡

Physical Therapy

Modern physiotherapy equipment — electrical stimulation, ultrasound, traction — to support recovery from musculoskeletal pain and dysfunction.

Learn More →
🌡️

Heat Therapy

Infrared and thermal devices warm deep tissue, improving circulation and relieving muscle tension. A gentle and comfortable treatment even for first-timers.

Learn More →
✨

Beauty Treatment

Facial care using Korean cosmetic acupuncture and pharmacopuncture to improve skin tone, boost elasticity, reduce puffiness, and minimize fine lines naturally.
