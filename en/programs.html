Our Services

Explore the full range of treatments at Bit Korean Medicine Clinic.
From pain relief to beauty care, we provide expert Korean medicine.

🪡 Acupuncture

Acupuncture (침 치료)

Acupuncture is a cornerstone of Korean medicine. Fine, sterile needles are inserted at specific acupoints distributed throughout the body to regulate the flow of Qi (vital energy) and blood. This unblocks stagnant meridians and activates the body's natural healing ability.

It is effective for a wide range of conditions including musculoskeletal pain (neck, lower back, knee, shoulder), headaches, digestive disorders, fatigue recovery, insomnia, and stress relief. Only single-use sterilized needles are used, ensuring complete hygiene and safety.

Musculoskeletal Pain Headache Digestive Disorders Insomnia Stress Relief
💉 Pharmacopuncture

Pharmacopuncture (약침 치료)

Pharmacopuncture involves injecting small amounts of refined herbal medicine extracts directly into acupoints. This combines the stimulating effect of acupuncture on the meridian system with the pharmacological properties of herbal medicine for enhanced therapeutic outcomes.

It is especially effective for musculoskeletal conditions such as arthritis, herniated discs, ligament injuries, and chronic pain. It aids in reducing inflammation and promoting tissue regeneration. Only purified, rigorously processed herbal extracts are used.

Arthritis Herniated Disc Ligament Injury Chronic Pain Anti-Inflammation
🤲 Chuna Therapy

Chuna Therapy (추나 요법)

Chuna Therapy is a Korean manual medicine technique in which the practitioner directly applies force to the spine, joints, and muscles to correct structural misalignments. It is not a simple massage — it is performed with precise anatomical knowledge and clinical expertise.

It is effective for cervical disc herniation, lumbar disc herniation, scoliosis, pelvic imbalance, and postural correction. Health insurance coverage is available, making treatment accessible at a reasonable cost.

Lumbar Disc Cervical Disc Scoliosis Postural Correction Insurance Covered
🔥 Cupping

Cupping Therapy (부항 치료)

Cupping therapy uses negative pressure to lift the skin and muscle layers, promoting blood circulation and assisting in the removal of metabolic waste and stagnant blood (blood stasis) from the body. It is performed in two forms: dry cupping and wet cupping.

It has excellent effects on relieving muscle fatigue and tension, shoulder and back stiffness, and improving circulation. It also supports immune function and overall fatigue recovery.

Muscle Fatigue Shoulder & Back Stiffness Blood Circulation Detoxification
⚡ Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy (물리 치료)

Physical therapy utilizes modern equipment including electrical stimulation (TENS/EMS), therapeutic ultrasound, spinal traction, and low/medium frequency therapy to assist recovery from musculoskeletal pain and restore physical function.

It is effective for both acute and chronic pain relief, recovery of injured muscles and ligaments, and reduction of swelling. When combined with acupuncture, faster recovery outcomes can be expected.

Electrotherapy Ultrasound Therapy Traction Therapy Pain Relief
🌡️ Heat Therapy

Heat Therapy (온열 치료)

Heat therapy uses infrared lamps, hot packs, and far-infrared devices to warm the painful area deep into the tissue, improving blood circulation and relaxing muscle tension. It provides deep, penetrating warmth that surface heat cannot reach.

Effective for muscle stiffness, joint rigidity, and chronic pain relief. When used as a preparatory step before acupuncture or Chuna therapy, it can enhance overall treatment effectiveness. A comfortable and gentle treatment even for first-time patients.

Muscle Relaxation Joint Stiffness Blood Circulation Chronic Pain
✨ Beauty Treatment

Beauty Treatment (미용 진료)

Korean cosmetic acupuncture and pharmacopuncture are used for facial beauty treatments. This approach improves skin health naturally, without synthetic chemicals or invasive procedures.

Effective for boosting skin elasticity, improving complexion, reducing facial puffiness, softening fine lines, and evening out skin tone. Since it harnesses the body's own healing capacity, side effects are minimal. Cumulative benefits build with regular sessions.

Skin Elasticity Complexion Puffiness Reduction Fine Lines Natural Beauty
