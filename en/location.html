How to Get Here

Directions

🚇 Subway

  • Line 1 Jegi-dong Station — approx. 3–5 min walk
  • Line 1 Cheongnyangni Station — approx. 8 min walk
  • Gyeongui·Jungang Line Cheongnyangni Station — approx. 8 min walk
  • After exiting the station, head toward Yangyeong Market

🚌 Bus

  • Alight at Cheongnyangni Station or Jegi-dong stop
  • Buses toward Yangyeong Market are available
  • For specific bus routes, please use Naver Maps or Google Maps

🚗 By Car

  • Navigation: search "Dongdaemun-gu Yagyeong-jungang-ro 9"
  • Paid parking lots are available near the building
  • We recommend calling ahead to confirm parking availability

🏥 Building Info

  • Located on the 2nd floor
  • Follow Yagyeong-jungang-ro and you will find us easily
  • For first-time visitors: check the directory board at the 1st floor entrance
